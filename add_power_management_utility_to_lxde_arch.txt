Display timeouts and power management can be added into LXDE via these packages:

sudo pacman -S xfce4-power-manager

Power Management utility is availible in LXDE Menu -> Preferences -> Power
Management

When you run it for the first time, it will ask, if you want to enable the power management service:
Click "Yes"
