how to create wifi access point on ubuntu

now go to Settings -> Network -> Wireless
click on "Use as Hotspot..." button
Click on "Options"
On "Wireless" tab change Mode from Ad-Hoc to Infrastructure
On "IPv4" tab change Method from Sharing to other computers to Automatic(DHCP)
Click Save

then you can connect for example an Android device to this wireless hotspot. use "Security Key" as your password.
